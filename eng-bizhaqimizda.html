Mission, Strategic Objectives, and Values

Mission – To satisfy customer demands to the maximum extent by offering high-quality and widely accessible financial services as a microfinance organization, and to be the best financial partner for our clients.

 

Strategic Objectives –

  • By the end of 2024, to open branches in every region of our country to swiftly and conveniently provide our services to all customers with demand for financial services;
  • Consistently strengthen our market position through the integration of innovations into our operations;
  • Ensure that asset efficiency remains above 30% to attract investors to our profitable business.

 

Our Values:

  • Transparency
  • Customer Focus
  • Professionalism and Promptness
  • Innovation and Reliability
  • Teamwork
